Aarah


Await a 40-minute scenic sea plane ride at the exclusive Heritance Aarah Lounge when you arrive in Male. Nestled within the scenic Raa Atoll, this Premium All-Inclusive resort in the Malsectiones sets the tone for the perfect getaway, with a myriad of offerings from an impressive array of dining options by award-winning chefs, to exploring the over or underwater marvels of the Raa Atoll. Responsible travel is at the heart of Heritance Aarah. We are the first LEED Gold certified building in the Malsectiones and the first green-rated building. We are also Travelife Gold certified and invite you to join our eco-initiatives whilst at the resort.

About Us

Heritance Hotel Hotel is a 5-star hotel located in Kandy, Sri Lanka. It is a luxurious hotel that offers spacious rooms, a spa, an outdoor pool, and a variety of dining options. The hotel is also home to a number of meeting and event spaces.
